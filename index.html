Freedom to Experience Fitness in a New Way

A new normal is descending upon all. With today’s ever-changing lifestyles, people are increasingly looking out for relevance and flexibility in their lifestyle choices. Fitness needs are no different.

We are reinventing the fitness industry with the pay-as-you-go payment option, empowering you the freedom to choose at will when and where you wish to exercise and at what price.

Download the Proxone app and register, available in both Android and iOS stores.

This unlocks the door to a world of Fitness, Exercise, Recreation, Sports and Adventure -available to seamlessly access, within your city and country.

The Proxone app lists a cross-section of activities you can access with a “click” giving you the freedom to experience fitness in a brand-new way.

Simply click the activity of your choice, based on the day and time slot you want to participate in and make your payment with a “click” to pay for the service utilizing a wide range of credit cards and electronic wallets.

No more is an annual membership the requirement to enter any of the Proxone activities. You pay only when you use each time. Decide on the activity you would like to workout at, check the date and the time slot which is available and make your payment. Once this booking is complete, on the facility provider’s backend your basic details will appear, with the date and time you will be attending the gym.

You can go 15 min before the scheduled time slot, scan the QR code on your phone and mark your attendance. By the time you change into your workout clothes, your slot would have begun. During your work out the trainer or host will be supporting you and helping you optimize your work out and guiding you as required. Once your time slot is over, you need to scan the QR code once more to mark the end of the workout.

A pop up will appear requesting you to star rate your experience and in some cases the individual trainers/instructors who were present during your slot. This helps to maintain the service standard offered at its optimum and your fitness experience to be always positive.
